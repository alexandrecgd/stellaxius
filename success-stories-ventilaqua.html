How VentilAQUA increased lead generation by 15x with Pardot marketing automation

VentilAQUA was able to increase organic traffic, lead generation and pipeline velocity with a new website and the implementation of Pardot and Sales Cloud for lead, opportunity and client management.

A company building technology to treat and save water

VentilAQUA is a 20-year-old company at the forefront of wastewater treatment technology development. Their main goals are to be the reference in wastewater treatment solutions for health and industrial sectors and to contribute to prevent black waters from contaminating our planet’s resources. And they are doing pretty well, as these days, VentilAQUA counts with highly efficient distributors across 22 countries and is proud to have more than 700 water treatment plants built for clients all around the world.

VentilAQUA’s Challenges

Despite the company’s know-how in wastewater decontamination and the high quality of their products, they were struggling with poor brand awareness and a low level of online lead generation. In fact, their main source of potential clients were the distributors and leads recommended by happy clients.

The urge for betting on an online strategy was real and Stellaxius accepted the challenge to develop a digital strategy to improve online brand awareness, brand authority and lead generation, alongside with the implementation of Pardot and Sales Cloud to support new lead, opportunity and client management processes, that would help VentilAQUA to better understand lead lifecycle, business pipeline and, ultimately, increase conversion.

How did Stellaxius helped?

By developing a new website for VentilAQUA and implementing marketing automation and CRM technology to improve and uniformize the lead, opportunity and client management processes across the company and among distributors.

Take a closer look below to understand the details.

Our Approach

The project was implemented using the Agile methodology and, throughout the sprints, Stellaxius developed the case for CRM and marketing automation implementation and configured the tools according to VentilAQUA’s needs.

A new website optimized for UX and conversion

Stellaxius helped VentilAQUA develop a new website fully optimized for user experience, search engines and conversion. In the new website information is structured in a user-friendly way with content that is relevant for the potential customer, increasing time in the website and decreasing bounce-rate.

New conversion points were added to promote content, such as case-studies and product information, capable of generating leads in every stage of the buying cycle, whether they are just searching for some information or already deciding which product to buy.

With a fully optimized website, VentilAQUA was able to increase in 48% organic search traffic, reduce bounce rate in 83% and increase the number of pages viewed per session by 30%.

Pardot as the marketing automation tool to support lead management

With the implementation of Pardot, VentilAQUA is now able to manage the lead lifecycle from lead generation to conversion, implementing new lead nurturing and opportunity follow-up strategies. Adding this to the use of a grading and scoring system to better qualify leads, the approach to new opportunities is better prioritized and pipeline velocity can be increased.

Using the Pardot and Salesforce connection, VentilAQUA is now able to manage leads and opportunities in Salesforce using engagement activity data from Pardot to leverage conversations with potential clients.

VentilAQUA is now using Salesforce campaigns to develop multi-touch campaign attribution reports to measure the success of each marketing initiative and the impact of marketing campaigns in lead generation and client conversion.

Using Sales Cloud for Opportunity and Account management

With Pardot and Sales Cloud integration, all Pardot information about lead qualification and engagement is shown in the lead profile in Salesforce. This allows for a more personalized sales approach and for sales people to know which leads or accounts are the most active and should be contacted first.

Salesforce is now the source of truth regarding client information, aggregating all leads by source and marketing initiative to be qualified and followed up by sales. In practice, VentilAQUA is using the automations from Pardot to assign leads and create tasks in Salesforce for qualified lead follow-up, in order to increase pipeline velocity.
