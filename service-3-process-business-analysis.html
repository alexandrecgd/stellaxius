Process & Business Analysis

It is all about strategy applied to business and technology.

What do we do?

It is our belief that a good alignment between business needs and technology is one of the most critical success factors for a successful delivery. We have the experience, knowledge and proven methodologies to promote this alignment and ensure IT teams deliver what brings the biggest business benefit first. Most organisations face problems with programs and projects that are not related to poor implementations or technology. Instead, the issues arise often from ineffective business or IT processes, coupled with a lack of trustworthy collaboration between Business and IT. This is where our certified business analysts teams come to play, way before a single piece of configuration is performed or a line of code is written.

Bring your Business & IT Together for Success with Efficient Demand Management

We help you build bridges between Business and IT with a tailored demand management process. Our methodology promotes the collaboration from all areas of your organisation, since everyone is able to express a business need and request a solution for it. All requests will then be prioritised, analysed and delivered, focusing always on your company's overall vision and strategy.

Demand Management Processes & Tools

We help you put into practice a demand process methodology, leveraging any procedures and policies you already have internally, with a focus on ensuring the demand process is measurable, efficient and beneficial to your users. If you are using Salesforce, we can implement our demand management tool in your instance, so that no idea is lost and your business needs are clear and structured for everyone.

Demand Prioritization Framework

When there are many different and conflicting needs, it is difficult to understand where to focus your resources. We can help you define a prioritisation framework that guarantees your stakeholders understand the decisions, the criteria behind it and, more importantly, that they work towards providing the relevant information to decide which ideas will have the biggest ROI.

Value Stream Mapping

As no process remains efficient, we found a way to adapt to IT and process analysis the known Lean methodology used in the automotive and other manufacturing industries. Our results are impressive and it works particularly well with an Agile delivery approach and a tool like Salesforce, enabling to constantly measure efficiency and remove waste.

Business Analysis to Understand and Solve your Business Issues

“Don't jump to conclusions” can also be rephrased to “Don't jump to implementation”. Understand your needs first, your main pain points, your biggest opportunities to grow, the “Why” and the “What” before the “How”. You help us to understand your business vision and goals and our certified business analysts will help you understand how you can do more and better with less.

Needs Assessment and Planning

We can help you pin point your business problems and opportunities, come up with a strategy, viable options and plan to enhance your current business state. This phase is crucial to validate that there is a sound motivation and the expected outcomes are positive for your organisation.

Elicitation and Analysis

We do not assume. We apply our joint knowledge and engage to gather all the relevant inputs. We then use all business analysis tools available to provide you with the solution that will achieve or exceed your expected business results.

Traceability, Monitoring and Evaluation

Business analysis ultimate goal is not a set of nicely documented business requirements. We follow through implementation and measure results to understand if the business benefits are being delivered and met. As solving the unknown is always more difficult, our tools are also helpful when a problem arises, enabling a quick trace back to its origin.

Business Process Analysis to Guide your Business Transformation

Business transformation is a vital and constant activity that organisations entail to grow, lead, remain competitive and stay ahead of their customers, employees and shareholders expectations. While simplification is at the heart of any transformation initiative, the reality is of increasing number of internal and external stakeholders to manage, with cross-functional responsibilities and goals. We help you bring clarity of your current business state, project the future and revisit your business processes in light of the new business models and technology options available.

As-Is and To-Be Business Process Design

When starting a transformation journey, it is as important to know where to go as it is to know where you are coming from. We develop a baseline view of the current state that will enable us to define the journey together, and identify what success looks like through metrics that we are able to correlate and compare to where you are now.

Business Process Reengineering and Automation

Organisations define processes, policies and procedures that are often rigid and ingrained into people's lives and systems features, making it very difficult to change. We unlock the power of technology to enable business process automation, and we help you reengineer your processes to continuously evolve and embrace change.

Cross-functional Business Process Management

There are many and often necessary interdependencies between business areas in an organization. While they share company goals, there may be conflicting when interacting with each other. We help you design cross-functional processes that improve the end-to-end outcome, with emphasis on optimized handovers and collaboration.

How do we help you?

Our projects contribute to solutions that enhance your business and not simply to a piece of software.

Understand Your Business

Before any implementation we ask you to help us understand your business, who you are as an organisation and most importantly what is your vision and strategy for the future. We make sure our projects are aligned with where you want to go and what success looks like when you get there.

Perform Business Analysis

With the context of your business, we start gathering and clarifying requirements with inputs received from multiple channels to get a complete view of your processes and needs. Every piece of analysis is documented and validated before it is ready to be handed over to the technical teams.

Validate and Track Benefits

Our business analysts are part of the delivery teams, acting as business proxies and facilitators. They control and monitor the implementation to ensure it is delivered to expectations. At the end of each deliverable, the KPIs set to be achieved are measured to ensure everyone is on track to exceed expectations and adjust where necessary.

Implement Demand Processes

A project has a beginning and an end, but not your organisational needs. Our Business and IT demand tool is put in place during the project to ensure that all your new needs, changes, and generated ideas flow through and get prioritised for future enhancements. This process will bring your Business and IT together in a single, prioritised and agreed product backlog.

Why should you work with us?

As companies grow, processes may not formally exist or may be created on top of other processes by different people, leading many times to non-efficient or undocumented ways of working. Our team of certified Business Analysts (IIBA-CBAP, PMI-PBA), are able to manage both the process and business analysis methodologies and tools required to generate the value your business is seeking. Therefore, by engaging on a project with us you can expect:

Higher levels of stakeholder engagement and contribution.

Clear, meaningful, prioritised and value-generating requirements.

The implementation of processes and tools that help you tackle future challenges.

Higher return on investment and end-customer satisfaction.
