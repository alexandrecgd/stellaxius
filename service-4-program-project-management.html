Program & Project Management

Manage your projects, tame the complexity.

What do we do?

A Project is a journey with a beginning and an end, where at the end you expect to be in a better place than when you started. It may be a simple definition but a very accurate one. A successful project in the other hand is when the journey finishes on time, within the budget, enjoying the safe ride and of course reaching your desired destination. We take project management very seriously and have certified project managers on different methodologies capable to define the best approach to each project. We have the experience to tailor and use best practices to multinational cross functional projects as to simple and fast implementations. Either way, the goal is always the same: reaching the end of the journey with a successful product.

Business & IT Delivery Management made simple

Organizations are complex and as they evolve and grow, there is a natural tendency for silos of information and different ways of working to arise leading to communication and collaboration issues. Your goals will be harder to achieve and the motivation of your staff will decrease. We know how to define and build an end to end operational model to get all your organisation to work together, with simple but effective processes.

Governance and Operating Model definition

Big or small every organisation needs to have a governance and operational model in place to coordinate the Business and IT delivery of new projects. We can help you define which governance and operating model best suits you, starting with a RACI exercise that will x-ray your ways of working and where they can be improved.

Project Management Office (PMO) Setup and Management

If you have several projects constantly running in your organisation, you should run them the same way using the methodology that best suits you. We can build you a PMO that will make each and everyone of your projects to run more efficiently and centralize your reporting on the status and benefits each project brings to your company.

Supplier Audit & Management

If you have a big implementation underway or about to start one and you realize you don't have the knowledge to monitor and audit the quality and benefits your supplier is providing, we can do that for you. We have been very successful at making sure our customers get their value for money.

Driving Performance across multiple projects

More and more organisations evolve through projects, they usually run in parallel and each of them should take you a step closer to your strategic goals. Coordination, prioritisation and making each individual project a success is pivotal for the growth of your organisation. We are very familiar to work on this type of environment and can help you making sure your investment is not going to waste.

Program & Portfolio Management

Following our Demand Management processes we can help you centralize, prioritise and manage different programmes, portfolios or product backlogs. We also help you decide which products should be developed first to achieve the biggest business benefits.

Project Management

We have certified project managers on different methodologies to manage your projects. We are more incline to advise you to follow more recent agile methodologies than waterfall approaches, however we are familiar with both and can manage your projects successfully either way.

Key Performance Metrics

As strange as it may be, it is not uncommon to see organisations implementing projects without setting KPIs before starting. A successful project needs to meet several criteria. Those need to be defined in the beggining of each project/programme. Our project managers and business analysts will make sure each of them are monitor and achieved.

Agile Transformation at your pace

In this digital age we live in, organisations need to be equipped with faster and effective ways to react to changes. We are proficient in delivering projects in any methodology but we do have a preference for agile project management as they can keep your focus on the business benefits first instead of admin or unnecessary bureaucracy. If you are still taking too long to react to our fast pace world, we have certified agile coaches that can, at your pace, help you shape your new agile organisation.

Organisational Transformation

Agile transformation is not an IT transformation. Your whole organisation needs to change and embrace this new way of working. We can help you drive this transformation across departments and up to the top and get all organisation on the same page.

Tools & Techniques Transfromation

We have coached big and small companies on their agile transformation, and big or small it all comes down to DRY (Dont Repeat Yourself). Each iteration needs to be better than the previous and this continuous improvement will lead you to success. We have the right tools and processes to help you setup this dynamic way to look at your projects.

Interpersonal Transformation

Agile transformation needs to be cross functional and across roles, but we cannot forget organisations are made of people. Changing people is the hardest part of agile transformation, our agile coaches have the right knowledge and techniques to teach and win-over the most reluctant employees of your organisation.

How do we help you?

Every project is different and no single approach to Project Management fits all.
Our focus is always to achieve the most efficient balance between the methodology and the internal customer guidelines for each implementation we are involved in.

Understand Your Methodology

We assess your organization's management processes and methodology to understand and measure how your teams are executing on them. We look at pain points and where you are successful, in light of your vision and strategic goals.

Propose Best Practice

Within the context of your organization, we map your current management processes and methodology to our best practice to identify gaps and propose quick-wins. We will focus on areas that will improve your pain points and leverage where you have been successful.

Facilitate Change

As transformation and change are difficult in nature, we will facilitate the transition by coaching and supporting your teams. We will apply collaboration techniques to ensure that your teams are the ones driving the change and we remain as invisible as possible.

Track Benefits Realization

The ultimate goal of Programme and Project Management is to ensure the expected benefits are realised. We will put processes and tools in place to define, measure and monitor the KPIs that everyone needs, so that they know if they are on track or need adjusting.

Why should you work with us?

We have trained and certified our team of Project Managers on both Traditional (PMI-PMP) and Agile (PMI-ACP, CSM) methodologies. We are equipped to apply the methodology and techniques that better fit your needs and how you work, adapting to and supporting transformation where needed. Therefore, by engaging on a project with us you can expect:

Industry and project management expertise.

Higher levels of stakeholder engagement and contribution.

The implementation of processes and tools that help you tackle future challenges.

Higher return on investment and less risk.
