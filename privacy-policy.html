Stellaxius commitment to data privacy protection

For Stellaxius, the protection and security of your personal data is a top priority. We make sure our business is conducted in compliance with the active laws on data privacy and security. With this policy we will help you understand what data may be collected from you, how it is going to be used and protected as well as with whom we may share it.

Throughout the website, you can navigate without revealing no information about yourself. However, to access more information we may require from you, your name as well as your e-mail for example, so as to process the information you have requested.

1. About your PeRsonal data


1.1 What kind of personal data do we collect?

When you navigate and interact with our website, we automatically collect information about your device and your usage of our website through cookies, web beacons or similar technologies, such as your IP address, which may qualify as Personal Data;

Other personal data, besides your IP address, is only collected with your consent. To access more information, download content or request a contact we may require from you some personal information, for example by requesting you to fill out a form. Personal data means any information the website users voluntarily provide about themselves. This information includes, but is not restricted to, name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, and information about user's company. Personal information may also include information about the users publicly available on the internet, namely in social media networks or other service providers.

When you identify yourself by giving us your email address, navigational information is collected. This means information about the user's computer and visits to Stellaxius website that may include, but is not restricted to, the user's IP address, browser information, referral source, geographical location, devices used, pages visited and length of visit.

1.2 NON-Personal Data we collect from other sources

While you access our website, we may, without the need of a formal registration, collect non-personal data (e.g. number of visits, average time spent on website, pages viewed) from other sources. This data, combined with personal data provided by you, will help us create a more tailored advertising and communication and better user experience.

1.3 Navigation and Usage Data we process

We also use web beacons in our website. For example, we may place web beacons in marketing emails that notify us when you click on a link in the email that directs you to our website. Such tracking technologies are used to operate and improve our websites and email communications and track the clicking of links or opening of emails.

Our website stores data on your computer in the form of a “cookie”, so we can recognize your PC next time you visit us. Cookies can be helpful, by tailoring a better experience which matches your interests. However, in case you do not want cookies to be stored on your pc, your browser can be set up to erase, block or even warn before cookies are stored.

Stellaxius uses Google Analytics, Pardot and Hotjar for tracking purposes to help us deliver a more relevant web experience to our users.

Google Analytics: This cookie collects information about, which website the visitor came from, number of individual user visits, the time spent browsing the website and how the user behaves on our website. The data is not recorded at the individual user level, it is only used to create aggregated web statistics.

Pardot: uses cookies to track users activities in order to recognize a returning visitor as a unique one. As such, Pardot uses cookies NOT to store personally identifying information in your computer but a unique identifier. The cookies are only readable by Pardot, and they will not attempt to access, read or modify data on your computer.

If you refuse the Pardot cookie, we will not be able to customize our website and our communication according to your preferences.

Hotjar: collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. These cookies do not personally identify anyone. If the cookies are disabled, Hotjar will not track any record from you.

In case you want to block or reject the cookies set by Stellaxius, you can do it by modifying your browser settings - if you are not sure how to do it, you can access the help function in your browser for additional details. Most browsers accept cookies by default so you need to actively delete or block them.

2. About the purpose of use

We will use the personal data that you provide to respond to your inquiries, requests and to keep in touch with you regarding Stellaxius. Any marketing communications can be opted-out by using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of emails. This will redirect you to a preferences page in which you can specify what communications you want to receive from us.

Stellaxius does not share, sell or rent to third parties any personal information about website users. All information provided will not be used for other commercial purposes other than Stellaxius information related (i.e. receive information about Stellaxius products and services).

The personal and navigational information collected from Stellaxius website users is used to:

  • Personalize users' experience on our website and other Stellaxius digital channels with personalized content and offers;
  • Send relevant information to the users via email, social media networks and other channels;
  • Send marketing communications related to Stellaxius products, services, events or promotions, as well as industry and company related content topics to the extent you have given your previous consent;
  • Handle contact requests, if you fill out a “Contact Us” form, we will process your Personal Data to the extent it is necessary for the purpose of our legitimate interests to fulfill your request and communicate with you;
  • We will process your Personal Data to analyze trends, track your usage of our website and interaction with emails to the extent this is necessary for our legitimate interests to develop and improve our websites and to provide our users with more relevant and interesting content;
  • We will process your Personal Data to conduct marketing research, advertise to you, provide personalized information about us on and off our website, and other personalized content based upon your activities and interests to the extent you have provided your prior consent for your personal data to be used for marketing purposes.

Customer testimonials and Success Use Cases available on Stellaxius website and communicated through other Stellaxius channels have due written authorization from the displayed data subject.

Security measures of Personal information collected via website are in place to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of Stellaxius website users' personal information. For queries regarding security of personal information, contact email privacy@stellaxius.com.

About Purpose Limitations

Stellaxius will collect, use or disclose personal data supplied by you online only for the purposes disclosed to you, unless:

  • any additional purpose that is a use of the personal data related to the original purpose the data was collected to
  • to prepare, negotiate and perform a contract with you
  • is necessary to communicate government or law authorities
  • preserve a legal claim our defense
  • prevent fraud and other illegal activities that compromise Stellaxius information technology systems

About Children

Stellaxius is not focused on children. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with Personal Data without your consent, please contact us as described in the “Contacting Us” section below and we will take steps to delete such Personal Data from our systems.

For How long do we keep your personal data?

Personal information that Stellaxius website users provide is retained for as long as it is considered useful to contacting users about Stellaxius products or services, or as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. All user's personal information will be deleted upon request from the user, unless we have legitimate interest.

About links to other websites

Our website have links to other websites which can have different privacy policies. Stellaxius is not responsible for the content displayed and privacy practices of those websites.

Therefore, we advise you to read the policy of each website you visit, it is rather important in case you provide sensitive information about yourself. Any concern or question you should enter in contact with the website manager or the legal department of that business.

About rights relating to your personal data

Stellaxius will review any personal data and make corrections or delete, upon reasonable request. The rights about your personal data, are subject to local data protection laws. These may include the following:

  • to access your Personal Data held by us (right to access);
  • to rectify inaccurate Personal Data and ensure it is complete (right to rectification);
  • to erase/delete your Personal Data to the extent permitted by other legal obligations (right to erasure; right to be forgotten);
  • to restrict our processing of your Personal Data (right to restriction of processing);
  • to transfer your Personal Data to another controller to the extent possible (right to data portability);
  • to object to any processing of your Personal Data carried out on the basis of our legitimate interests (right to object). Where we process your Personal Data for direct marketing purposes or share it with third parties for their own direct marketing purposes, you can exercise your right to object at any time to such processing without having to provide any specific reason for such objection;
  • not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects ("Automated Decision-Making"); Automated Decision-Making currently does not take place on our websites;
  • to the extent we base the collection, processing and sharing of your Personal Data on your consent, to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of the processing based on such consent before its withdrawal.

About Questions and comments

You can contact us to make questions or comments, by sending an email to privacy@stellaxius.com.
