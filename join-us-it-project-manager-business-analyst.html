Job Description

As an IT Project Manager / Business Analyst, you will lead the analysis effort on significant and often multiple business units in local and international projects. You will be a confident, articulate communicator who can develop and maintain effective working relationships with a wide range of stakeholders.

This opportunity would best suit proactive, adaptable individuals with skills and experience in using a wide range of project management and business analysis tools and techniques, who are keen to develop their careers in a fast-paced, customer-focussed environment.

Responsibilities

  • Capture, document, facilitate and manage business need (including shaping and scoping ideas, aligning to business strategy and business goals, eliciting business requirements, and ensuring traceability of requirements throughout the project lifecycle)
  • Accurately assess business architecture impact (including the impact of change on existing process, organisation and technology, capturing and documenting new and updated business processes)
  • Conduct meetings, prepare business process documentation and process flows
  • Architect and design solutions to business needs
  • Coordinate and follow-up end-to-end the delivery of the business requirements
  • Work closely with different internal teams and other parties

Skills

  • Knowledge on Waterfall and Agile approaches and be capable on applying both
  • Strong stakeholder management
  • Ability to translate the requirements into successful projects
  • CRM knowledge (preferable Salesforce)

Personal Skills

  • Strong analytical skills
  • Strong communication skills with the ability to communicate on a technical and project management level
  • Resilient
  • Autonomous
  • Proactive
  • Teamwork
  • Ability to apply new concepts, business models, and technologies

Desirable

  • We value any of the following certifications (or similar): PMP; CSM, PMI-ACP; PMI-BPA; CBAP; Salesforce certifications
  • Available for travelling

Languages

  • English fluent spoken and written ability
  • Other multi-lingual skills are a plus

Education

  • University degree level

What do we have to offer

  • Personalised training and certification path
  • A stable and dynamic working environment where you can develop
  • The possibility to come up with ideas, be listened and see them implemented
  • A team where work becomes fun
  • Happy Hour every Friday
