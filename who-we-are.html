Who We Are

We help you transform your business by solving your most complex challenges

We are a group of individuals who loves working together and solve your most complex challenges. For us every project is about fostering a close collaboration culture that empowers our customers and our employees to share goals, working together to transform your business and maximize Return on Investment.

At Stellaxius, we are focused on turning our clients into superheroes by offering you simple-to-use consulting services and implementing sustainable solutions that transform people through technology and knowledge. We believe success comes with strong human relations to help transform businesses, improve people's lives and have an impact on our community.


Why would you work with us?


In our Stellaxius community, we are all addicted to TETRIS, not the game, but a set of core values shared by our team, our partners and clients.

Teamwork

We join the energy from customers, partners and employees to tackle real business challenges.

Expertise

We are always learning, coaching and sharing knowledge within and outside the team.

Trust

We value long-lasting relationships with everyone involved in the business, respecting and building upon different views.

Responsibility

We aspire to drive change, with proactivity and always seeking knowledge while being aware of our actions.

Innovation

We embrace change in processes, technology and people, fostering a continuous innovation environment.

Satisfaction

We take pride in delivering high quality and above customer expectations solutions, challenging everyone involved to do their best

TETRIS is a game that was created to “make computers less intimidating” according to its founder and has become timeless. This is exactly what we want to do for our clients, by helping businesses embracing technology and transformation seamlessly. We are experts at simplifying business digital transformation with simple-to-use solutions that fit your needs regardless of the size or complexity of the project, empowering people and growing with your business in the long-term.
