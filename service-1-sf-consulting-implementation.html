Salesforce Consulting & Implementation

Leverage your Business with Salesforce Technology

What do we do?

We are official Salesforce partners, specialized in implementing Salesforce-based solutions adapted to your business requirements. We provide you with solutions that focus on quick-wins to solve your business issues. We have a deep knowledge of the Salesforce products landscape, technical expertise and experience in Business Analysis and a Consulting practice in a variety of industries, including but not limited to telecommunications, finance, insurance and utilities.

Revolutionize your Sales Process

We help you implement and configure Salesforce products to increase your sales team productivity and results. With Salesforce Sales Cloud, the #1 CRM solution in the world, you will be able to easily manage and control your sales pipeline, leads and opportunities. By joining Salesforce Einstein you will be able to forecast sales, use intelligent automation and get the analytics you're missing to measure results.

Salesforce Sales Cloud

We help you implement and configure Salesforce products to increase your team's productivity by spending less time doing non-selling activities.

Salesforce CPQ

Can be customized to help your sales representatives to generate accurate quotes, proposals and contracts easier and faster.

Salesforce Sales Cloud Einstein

With the implementation of Einstein in your Sales Cloud, we help you get the analytics you're missing to forecast your sales, prioritize your leads and follow up on opportunities with intelligent automation.

Transform your Marketing Approach

We implement Salesforce Marketing Products to help you collect consumer insights, personalize communication and measure Marketing ROI.

Salesforce Marketing Cloud

Marketing Cloud help you integrate all touchpoints to build 1-to-1 customer journeys and use AI to discover segments and drive engagement. With Einstein you can act on insights and measure ROI.

Salesforce Pardot

Salesforce B2B marketing automation solution allow you to nurture your leads with intelligent automation, target your message with dynamic content, analyze your prospect's activity and measure marketing revenue.

Salesforce DMP

Salesforce solution to capture and unify customer data, segment your target audience precisely with help of AI and reach customers based on their individual footprint across touchpoints.

Enhance your Service and Customer Support

By implementing Salesforce Service solutions applied to your business, you can deliver the ultimate customer experience by helping your team anticipate your customers' needs and address them faster and more accurately.

Salesforce Service Cloud

Implementing Salesforce Service Cloud will help you improve customer satisfaction, by giving agents a deeper understanding of customers and boost efficiency by making it easier for them to follow support cases. We configure your service cloud to help you measure your support team performance with analytics and reduce case volume with a branded self-service portal with peer-shared knowledge.

CTI for Salesforce

Allows you to integrate your phone system with your CRM. The integration with the CRM, helps sales reps to personalize their calls and service agents to reduce call handle times and increase first call resolution rates by providing the information they need to resolve cases.

How do we help you?

Every project we are involved in follows a methodology to achieve maximum return on your investment:

Discovery and Envisioning

We analyze your business to assess how we can help with your digital transformation goals and strategic objectives, and define KPIs. Based on this we develop your business case for the change, define project scope and forecast your performance after the transformation. There is no contract obligation at this stage.

Iterative Configuration

We start configuring your solution, in what we call Sprints, leveraging standard features and mapping your business model to Salesforce. We then iteratively configure and customize to your specific needs, following our AGILE methodology to address the most valuable needs first.

Training your Staff

The last stage aims at training your team to ensure the technology is adopted within your organization. Our program includes an adoption strategy, focused on facilitating the change of business processes, measuring and validating the results derived from the adoption of the technology.

Validation and Adaptation

Every iteration going live includes a process to train your team and ensure the technology is adopted within your organization. Following an agreed adoption strategy, we facilitate change to business processes, measuring and validating the results derived from the transformation.

Why should you work with us?

We have a team of professionals with 10+ years of experience in the Salesforce ecosystem. To ensure project success, we keep our team updated and certified in Salesforce and Project Management techniques. Therefore, by engaging on a project with us you can expect:

Industry and product expertise.

Higher levels of technology adoption.

The implementation of processes and tools that help you tackle future business challenges.

Higher return on investment and less time to achieve break-even.
